About Me

Hello! My name is Guilherme, and I'm a 24 y/o IT student based in Porto Alegre, Brazil. I'm currently available for freelance opportunities.


In my spare time, I enjoy reading, gaming, hanging out with friends, watching shows and movies, building stuff with code, and studying new technologies related to programming. My goal is to become a successful web developer.

Why Web Development?

I've been drawn to creative fields for as long as I can remember. My childhood interest eventually moved on from pen and paper to the computer screen, where I dabbled in basic HTML and pixel art during my teenage years.

In late 2021, I immersed myself in an IT course, where I learned how to create and design an website from scratch. By 2023, I had transitioned from the technical course to a college program focused on analysis and system development, solidifying my passion for web development.
