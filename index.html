About Me

Hello! My name is Guilherme, and I'm a 24 y/o IT student based in Porto Alegre, Brazil. I'm currently available for freelance opportunities.


In my spare time, I enjoy reading, gaming, hanging out with friends, watching shows and movies, building stuff with code and studying new technologies related to programming. My goal is to become a successful Front-End Developer.

Why Front-End?

I've been drawn to creative fields for as long as I can remember. My childhood interest eventually moved on from pen and paper to the computer screen, where I dabbled on basic HTML and pixel art during my teenage years.

Near the end of 2021, I dove headfirst into what truly interested me and enrolled into an IT course. Learning how to build the "face" of a website sparked my curiosity and satisfied my creative itch like nothing before. As of 2023, I realized my interest for web development had already evolved into passion.
